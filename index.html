Daily Survivor Contest

Open Now

All Survivor Challengers can submit their daily Survivor pick using the entry form before tip off each day of play.

Survivor Challenge Rules:

  • Up to two entries max per member.
  • Pick One team each day of tournament play.
  • Only teams playing that day can be picked.
  • Once a team has been picked, it cannot be picked again.
  • Daily deadline is tip-off of the first game that day.
  • No pick = immediate loss.
  • If your team wins, you advance. If not, you are eliminated.
  • Tiebreaker: total seed points across picks days 1–10.
  • If you didn’t join last year, don’t miss your chance to play & win this year!
Submit your Survivor pick before the first game tips off today.

Submit your pick using the official Google Form. After submission, you will see a confirmation message: Your Survivor pick has been submitted.
