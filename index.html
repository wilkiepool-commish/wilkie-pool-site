Survivor Contest

Open Now

Survivor is a side pool challenge that begins Day 1 of the tournament.
All Survivor Challengers can submit their daily Survivor pick using the official entry form before tip off each day of play.

Survivor Challenge Rules:

  • $10/entry - Up to two entries max per member.
  • Pick One team each day of tournament play.
  • Only teams playing that day can be picked.
  • Once a team has been picked, it cannot be picked again.
  • Daily deadline is tip-off of the first game that day.
  • No pick = immediate loss.
  • If your team wins, you advance. If not, you are eliminated.
  • Tiebreaker: total seed points across picks days 1–10.
  • If you didn’t join last year, don’t miss your chance to play & win this year!
Submit your Survivor pick before the first game tips off today.

Submit your pick using the official Google Form. After submission, you will see a confirmation message: Your Survivor pick has been submitted.

Sweet 16 Reset

Opens Round of 16

Sweet 16 Reset is a side pool challenge that begins at the Round of 16 during the tournament.

Sweet 16 Rules:

  • Members pick 15 games of the NCAA Tournament, starting with the Sweet 16 round.
  • The pick deadline is set to March 26, 2026 at 6:59 PM ET - PRIOR TO FIRST TIP-OFF.
  • Each participant is limited to a single entry ($15) in the contest.
  • Points are awarded for each correct pick.
  • Points values increase as the tournament progresses, as follows:
    • Sweet 16 = 4 points
    • Regional Final (Elite 8) = 8 points
    • National Semifinal (Final 4) = 16 points
    • Championship = 32 points
  • The pool does not use a seed point bonus. Correct picks will be awarded the points assigned for each round.
  • The tie-breaker is the combined number of points scored in the Championship game. Each member must enter a tie-break value when they make their picks.
  • The winner is the entry with the most points at the end of the NCAA Tournament.
