Survivor Contest

Open Now

Survivor is a side pool challenge that begins Day 1 of the tournament and runs until a champion is crowned.
All Survivor Challengers can submit their Survivor pick the day of using the official entry form below. Remember to make your picks before tip off each day of play.

Survivor Challenge Rules:

  • Each entry is $10 - Up to two (2) entries max, per contestant. (This is a side pool and does not include your main bracket entry)
  • Contestants will select one (1) team each day of tournament play. (10 days total = 10 picks total)
  • Only teams playing that same day can be picked.
  • Once a team has been picked, it cannot be picked again for the duration of the tournament. (Your Survivor challenge journey ends if you have no remaining teams available to choose from)
  • Survivor pick deadline is tip-off of the first game, that day of tournament play. All picks are locked in shortly after tip and will be posted to website shortly thereafter.
  • Failure to submit your pick on time will result in an immediate loss. Picks cannot be changed after tip-off.
  • Win to advance to the next round. Loss = elimination.
  • In the event of a tiebreaker, 1 point will be awarded to highest total of seeds chosen, between all tied parties. (It pays to pick upsets!)
  • Payment for Survivor Contest entry is due immediately upon entry. Pay before games begin Thursday at Noon to confirm entry.
If you didn’t join last year, don’t miss your chance to play & win this year!

Submit your picks using the official Google Form. After submission, you will see a confirmation message: "Your Survivor pick has been submitted."

Sweet 16 Reset

Open Now

Sweet 16 Reset is a side pool challenge that begins at the Round of 16 during the tournament, running through the championship game.

Sweet 16 Rules:

  • Reset your busted brackets. Entrants pick the remaining 15 games of the NCAA Tournament, starting with the Round of 16.
  • The pick deadline is set to March 26, 2026 at 6:59 PM ET - Prior to first tip-off.
  • Each entrant is limited to a single entry ($15) in the contest.
  • Points are awarded for each correct pick.
  • Points values increase as the tournament progresses, as follows:
    • Sweet 16 = 4 points
    • Regional Final (Elite 8) = 8 points
    • National Semifinal (Final 4) = 16 points
    • Championship = 32 points
  • The pool does not use a seed point bonus. Correct picks will be awarded the points assigned for each round.
  • The tie-breaker is the combined number of points scored in the Championship game. Each member must enter a tie-break value when they make their picks.
  • The winner is the entry with the most points at the end of the NCAA Tournament.
Reset your busted bracket for another shot at the top when the Sweet 16 tips off.

Submit your picks using the official Google Form. After submission, you will see a confirmation message: "Your Sweet 16 picks have been submitted."
